Scallion Chicken

Ingredients

    5 chicken thigh, boneless with the skin on 4 stalks green onion, chopped 2.5 tablespoon soy sauce 3 tablespoon oyster sauce 1 tablespoon honey A pinch of black pepper 1.5 tablespoon cornstarch 2 tablespoon Shaoxing Wine (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and marinade with soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, black pepper, and corn starchâ€”massage and set aside for 5 minutes while you prepare the rest of the ingredients.
  2. Chop green onion and separate it with the whites and the greens.
  3. In a pan, drizzle in a bit of oil, and turn the heat to medium-high. Add in the chicken thigh and set it for 1 minute before pan-frying for another 4-5 minutes.
  4. Pour in Shaoxing Wine, beer, or water, and put the lid on to cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add in the rest of the green onion, turn the heat to high, and saute on high heat for another 1-2 minutes.
